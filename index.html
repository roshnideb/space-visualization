For this project, I have designed a creative data visualization centered around global space-related activities to foster deeper appreciation for space exploration. The moving spheres represent space-related articles, which emerge from the Earth at the location associated with the article. The color is determined by its category, which is shown in the legend. You will be able to change the sizes of the categories using the circles in the legend. You can also rotate the earth with the mouse.

Visualizing space-related activities across the world
from 2015 to 2023
